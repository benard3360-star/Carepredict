About CarePredict

Revolutionizing healthcare workforce planning through advanced machine learning and predictive analytics

Our Mission

CarePredict is designed to transform how healthcare institutions identify and nurture future caregiving professionals. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, we analyze comprehensive student data to predict the likelihood of successful transition into caregiving roles.

Our goal is to optimize workforce planning, improve training programs, and ensure that healthcare institutions can effectively identify and support students who will become successful caregivers.

Our Methodology

Our prediction model uses a sophisticated CatBoost algorithm trained on comprehensive student data to deliver highly accurate predictions.

Data Collection

We analyze 14 key features including academic performance, demographics, practical assessments, and training metrics to build comprehensive student profiles.

Machine Learning

Our CatBoost model processes categorical and numerical data to identify patterns and relationships that predict care transition success.

Validation

The model has been rigorously tested and validated to achieve 95% accuracy in predicting care transition outcomes.

Key Features Analyzed

Academic Performance

Final grades, theory exams, and overall academic standing

Practical Assessments

Hands-on evaluations and practical skill demonstrations

Attendance & Engagement

Class attendance rates and participation levels

Hospital Internships

Internship performance and real-world experience scores

Course Duration

Length of training program and completion rates

Demographics

Geographic location and educational background
